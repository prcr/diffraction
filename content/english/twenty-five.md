feel that christmas spirit  
covering us all  
with calm silence

the lonely wind fiddles  
with the tree's decorations  
while, in the distance,  
snow flakes reflect lost hymns

...makes me wish  
i could fly away  
up to the cold pole  
leaving the blinking joy  
and color celebrating behind.  
   
   
*(1998)*
